Fall Sports

Football

Football is a contact team sport played on a rectangular field where the goal is to score points by advancing the ball into the opposing end zone. Played with 11 players per team on the field.

Cross Country

Cross country is a distance running sport typically raced over natural terrain such as grass or dirt. Teams compete for fastest times, usually 7 runners per team.

Volleyball

Volleyball is a non-contact sport where two teams of 6 try to ground the ball on the opposing team’s side of the court over a net.

Soccer (Boys)

Boys' soccer is a globally popular sport where two teams of 11 compete to score goals by moving a ball into the opponent’s net using their feet.

Winter Sports

Basketball

Basketball is played indoors with 5 players per team. Teams score by shooting a ball through the opposing team’s hoop.

Wrestling

Wrestling is an individual sport where two competitors attempt to pin each other to the mat. Team scores are based on individual match wins.

Indoor Track

Indoor track includes various short and long-distance events, relays, and field events. Team size varies, events are individual or in small teams.

Swimming

Swimming is an individual and relay sport held in a pool. Events include freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke, and butterfly. Team sizes vary by event.

Spring Sports

Baseball

Baseball is a bat-and-ball sport played between two teams of 9 players. The goal is to score runs by hitting and running bases.

Softball

Softball is similar to baseball, played with a larger ball and underhand pitching. Teams also have 9 players on the field.

Track and Field

This sport combines running, jumping, and throwing events. Athletes compete individually and in relays. Team sizes vary greatly.

Soccer (Girls)

Girls' soccer follows the same rules as boys', with 11 players per team. Matches are 90 minutes, with teams aiming to score by kicking the ball into the opponent's net.

Summer Sports

Summer Training

Off-season training sessions open to all athletes for conditioning and skill-building. Team sizes vary and depend on sport participation.

Summer League Basketball

Summer basketball leagues offer continued play outside school seasons. Typically 5 players per team on the court.

Soccer Clinics

Short-term training camps focusing on skill development in soccer. No fixed team size, sessions are run by coaches or clubs.

Swim Club

Summer swim clubs are community-based and offer meets for individual and team-based races. Participants vary by age and ability.
